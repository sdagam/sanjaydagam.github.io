About This Blog

Mathematics becomes truly exciting when you see how it powers the world around us. Each article connects a core concept to a real application — written for students with an AP Calculus background who want to explore what comes next.

The series follows a deliberate progression: foundations in 2024 → engineering depth in late 2024 → linear algebra in 2025 → differential equations and real analysis in 2026–2027.
